The '''spirit tree & fairy ring''' can be built in the [[teleport space]] of the [[superior garden]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 95 [[Construction]], 83 [[Farming]], and a [[watering can]] to build and when built, it gives 885 Construction and Farming [[experience]]. Players can teleport to other [[fairy rings]] and [[spirit trees]] using this teleportation space. Other players can use the tree-ring, provided they have met the requirements to do so. This does not count towards the number of spirit trees a player may own.

A set of [[goblin decorations]] can be used on the tree to make a Christmas-themed spiritual fairy tree, or a [[Raging echoes spirit tree scroll]] to make a [[Raging Echoes League]]-themed tree. Using a [[spade]] on the tree while in building mode allows the player to rotate it. The [[Raging echoes spirit tree scroll]] can be removed by attempting to remove the spirit tree in build mode.

The minimum levels a spirit tree & fairy ring can be built at are 90 Construction (with use of a [[spicy stew]]) and 78 Farming (also with the use of a spicy stew). A [[crystal saw]] cannot be used. Otherwise, the alternative is making 2 superior gardens and planting the [[Spirit tree (Construction)|tree]] and [[Fairy ring (Construction)|fairy ring]] separately, which requires level 75 Construction and 85 Construction respectively.

Compared with an ordinary [[Fairy ring (Construction)|fairy ring]] in a player-owned-house, the spirit tree & fairy ring does not have a right-click favourites menu. The loss of this feature may be a reason to prefer a separate ring and tree as opposed to the combined version.
