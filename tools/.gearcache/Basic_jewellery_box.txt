The '''basic jewellery box''' can be built in the [[jewellery box space]] of the [[achievement gallery]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 81 [[Construction]] to build and gives 605 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it.

The basic jewellery box is often built as a cheaper and easier alternative to replenish health, prayer, and run energy (but not special attack) than a [[fancy rejuvenation pool]] or [[desert amulet 4]]; due to the ability to use the [[Ferox Enclave]] teleport provided by the [[ring of dueling]] to allow easy access to the [[free-for-all portal]] and the [[Pool of Refreshment|pool of refreshment]]. The lowest level a basic jewellery box can be made is level 75 Construction, as level 80 Construction is needed to build the achievement gallery with a +5 boost from a [[spicy stew]]. The +3 boost from a [[crystal saw]] cannot be used to build the achievement gallery, however this boost can be used to build any level of jewellery box.
