The '''occult altar''' can be built in the [[Altar_space#Achievement_Gallery|altar space]] of the [[achievement gallery]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 90 [[Construction]] to build and gives 3,445 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it.

Players can switch their spellbook to one of the following: the [[Arceuus spellbook]], [[Lunar spellbook]], [[Standard spells|standard spellbook]], or [[Ancient Magicks]]. Other players can use the altar, provided they have the requirements to access those spellbooks.

In order to build the occult altar, players must right-click upgrade from the altar they have built, with the required materials dependent on the altar built. For example, upgrading the [[Ancient altar]] to the occult altar will require 1 [[lunar signet]], 10,000 [[astral rune]]s, 1 [[Arceuus signet]], 5,000 [[blood rune]]s, and 5,000 [[soul rune]]s.

The [[crystal saw]] boost is applicable, making the minimum requirement level 82 Construction with a +5 boost from an orange [[spicy stew]].

For the Dark altar and Lunar altar, although the recipes list that [[Pharaoh's sceptre (uncharged)]] can be used, a sceptre with any charge level will be acceptable.

==Trivia==
* During development, the altar was called "Altar of the Gods". It was presumably changed as only the [[Ancient Magicks]] are associated with a [[god]] — [[Zaros]].
* The books on the altar contain Morse code, which can be translated, from left to right, as: "cosmic comic" in the [[Lunar altar|Lunar book]]; and "darkaess{{sic}} corrupts" in the [[Dark altar (Construction)|Dark book]]. 
** The misspelling of "darkness" may be an intentional reference to [[Arceuus]], as it is pronounced in a similar fashion — ''(dar-KAY-us)''. 
** The "A" (<span style="font-family: monospace; font-size: 40%; vertical-align: 3ex; white-space: pre-wrap;">&#9604; &#9604;&#9604;&#9604;</span>) may simply be a mirrored misspelling of "N" (<span style="font-family: monospace; font-size: 40%; vertical-align: 3ex; white-space: pre-wrap;">&#9604;&#9604;&#9604; &#9604;</span>).
* Runes in a rune pouch do not count toward the requirement to build this item. They must be moved from the pouch to the inventory.
