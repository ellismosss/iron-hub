The '''crystalline portal nexus''' can be built in the [[portal nexus space]] of the [[portal nexus]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 92 [[Construction]] to build and gives 2,600 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it, meaning it can be built at 84 construction through the use of a [[crystal saw]] and a +5 [[spicy stew]] boost.

The crystalline portal nexus can be directed to '''forty-one''' locations of a player's choosing, in which each destination directed requires 1,000 times the required [[runes]] for casting the [[teleport]] spell. Refer to the [[Portal nexus#Adding a teleport|Adding a teleport]] page for estimated rune cost per teleport and relevant information on upgrading.

The crystalline portal nexus can be redecorated into a [[Raging Echoes League]]-themed portal nexus by using the [[Raging echoes nexus scroll]] on it.
