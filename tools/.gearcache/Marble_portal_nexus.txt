The '''marble portal nexus''' can be built in the [[portal nexus space]] of the [[portal nexus]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 72 [[Construction]] to build and gives 2,000 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it. The earliest a Portal Nexus room can be built is at 67 Construction using only the +5 boost from spicy stew, as the crystal saw does not affect the levels required to construct rooms.

The marble portal nexus can be directed up to '''four''' locations of a player's choosing; to do so, a payment of 1,000 times the required [[runes]] for casting the [[teleport]] spell is required for each directed teleportation (some teleport spells cannot be directed). Additional information about adding and customising the portal nexus can be found [[Portal_nexus#Adding_a_teleport|here]].
