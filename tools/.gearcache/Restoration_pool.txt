The '''restoration pool''' can be built in the [[pool space]] of the [[superior garden]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 65 [[Construction]] to build and gives 706 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it, and the items cannot be noted. The lowest Construction level required to build the pool is 60 with the use of a +5 [[Temporary skill boost|boost]] from a [[spicy stew]] due to the superior garden requiring 65 and the same spicy stew boost. Rooms in player-owned houses cannot be built with the [[crystal saw]]'s boost.

Players can restore their [[Special attacks|special attack]] energy using this pool. This cannot be done if the player was recently in [[Player killing|PvP]] combat; attempting to do so produces the message, ''"You can't drink from the pool during combat."''

The [[goblin decorations]] can be used on the pool to make a frozen restoration pool; note the decorations must be removed before upgrading the pools.
