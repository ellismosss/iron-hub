The '''fancy rejuvenation pool''' can be built in the [[pool space]] of the [[superior garden]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 85 [[Construction]] (or 77 with a [[crystal saw]] and a [[spicy stew]]) to upgrade the [[rejuvenation pool]]. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it, which gives 1,950 [[Construction]] [[experience]].

Players can restore their [[Special attacks|special attack]], [[Energy|run energy]], [[prayer points]], and any reduced [[stats]] (excluding [[Hitpoints]]) using this pool. This cannot be done if the player was recently in [[Player killing|PvP]] combat; attempting to do so produces the message, ''"You can't drink from the pool during combat."''

The [[goblin decorations]] can be used on the pool to make a frozen fancy rejuvenation pool; note the decorations must be removed before upgrading the pools.
