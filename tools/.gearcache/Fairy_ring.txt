The '''fairy ring''' transportation system is unlocked by [[members]] after starting the [[Fairytale II - Cure a Queen]] quest and getting permission from the [[Fairy Godfather]]. It consists of 55 teleportation rings spread across the world and provides a relatively fast means of accessing often remote sites in [[Gielinor]], as well as providing easy access to other realms.

Players can access the fairy ring system from any fairy ring while wielding either a [[dramen staff]] or [[lunar staff]]. From there, using three-letter codes, they can teleport to most rings within the system. If the [[Lumbridge & Draynor Diary]] is fully completed, no staff of either kind is needed. Several rings, however, require players to complete quests before they can be accessed. If the selected code has no corresponding fairy ring or is a fairy ring the player is already located in, players will not be teleported anywhere, exclaiming "Wow, fairy magic sure is useful, I hardly moved at all!".

Players with level 85 in [[Construction]] can build a [[Fairy ring (Construction)|fairy ring]] in their [[superior garden]].

Note that [[Fairytale I - Growing Pains]] '''must''' be completed to access the fairy rings. [[Fairytale II - Cure a Queen]] '''does not''' need to be completed, and players do not need any of the skill requirements.

When using the fairy rings, a travel log will pop up. This is a list of rings to which you have recently travelled. This is useful, as you can just click on the ring code in the travel log and the code will be automatically entered. Up to ten fairy ring codes can be marked as favourites for quicker code dialling to frequented rings.

The fairy ring system was seemingly created by the [[Fairy (race)|fairies]] through fairy magic, as a means of transportation between planes and across the overworld. The rings are imbued with a protective magic that prevents you from being attacked by monsters when standing in the centre of them.{{CiteNPC|npc=Co-ordinator|quote=Fortunately the fairy rings are imbued with a powerful protective magic and as long as you stand in the middle of them nothing will be able to attack you.|loc=Fairytale II - Cure a Queen|notes=|name=}}

==Closest points of interest to fairy rings==
* Bank - Chasm of Fire {{Fairycode|djr}} (29 tiles) to a Shayzien bank chest.
**Players that have both a [[Spirit tree (Construction)|Spirit Tree]] and fairy ring in their POH may use the [[Farming Guild]] for quicker bank access. This requires 85 [[Farming]], 85 [[Construction]], and a Spirit Tree in the Farming Guild.
*Altar - Mudskipper Point {{Fairycode|aiq}} (84 tiles) (or POH chapel/pool via {{Fairycode|diq}} if this has been built in the [[superior garden]]).
**After completion of [[Children of the Sun]], the altar in Outer Fortis is slightly closer via the {{Fairycode|ajp}} ring (65 tiles).
**Alternatively, one can use {{Fairycode|bip}} to reach the Nature grotto altar with 50 Agility (54 tiles and a shortcut). This altar also boosts your prayer by two points over your level.
*Sand pit - Zanaris (~30 tiles)
*Tool Leprechaun - {{Fairycode|djp}} (43 tiles). An [[Ardougne cloak 1]] or higher is a superior alternative at 14 tiles.
**{{Fairycode|akr}} (34 tiles) after visiting [[Great Kourend]] at least once.
**{{Fairycode|cks}} (10 tiles) after completion of [[Priest in Peril]].
**{{Fairycode|ckq}} (6 tiles) after completion of [[Children of the Sun]].

''Note: Tiles are measured without diagonal steps and may be more or less based on teleport deviation.''

==Trivia==
*When attempting to use the fairy ring network without completion of the [[Lumbridge & Draynor Diary#Elite|elite Lumbridge & Draynor diary]], a [[dramen staff]], or a [[lunar staff]], players receive the message {{mes|The fairy rings only work for those who wield fairy magic.}}
*If the player has trapped themselves at a location only accessible by the fairy ring network, they may teleport back to Zanaris or from Zanaris to the Lumbridge Swamp without a dramen or lunar staff regardless of if they have the elite Lumbridge & Draynor diary completed.
*When using a fairy ring, any item equipped in the off-hand slot will momentarily disappear.
*Prior to the [[Update:Diverse Dungeons|Diverse Dungeons]] update, fairy ring code {{Fairycode|DJR}} teleported the player to [[Sinclair Mansion]] (west).
*Fairy rings {{Fairycode|AJS}}, {{Fairycode|AKQ}}, {{Fairycode|BIQ}}/{{Fairycode|DLQ}}, {{Fairycode|DIP}}, {{Fairycode|CLR}}, {{Fairycode|DLR}} and {{Fairycode|DIQ}} have a 1/6 chance of spawning a [[Penguin (monster)|penguin]], [[Chinchompa (Hunter)|grey chinchompa]], [[jackal]], unattackable [[Spawn|Abyssal Sire spawn]], unattackable [[small ninja monkey]], [[badger]] and [[servant]], respectively, when teleporting away from those fairy rings to make it look as if they came through the teleport with you.{{CiteTwitter|author=Mod Ash|url=https://twitter.com/JagexAsh/status/1364553015022256128|date=24 February 2021|archiveurl=https://reldo.runescape.wiki/i/97813|archivedate=5 March 2022|quote=[AJS, AKQ and BIQ fairy ring has a small chance of spawning either a Penguin, Grey Chin or Jackal] We also did it for abyssal creatures from the Abyssal Nexus, monkeys from Ape Atoll, badgers from the Zulrah telescope island, and POH butlers.
The small chance is 1/6, by the way.|name=Fairy ring teleport NPC spawns}}
* There are only 64 possible combinations using 4 letters on each ring, meaning that at some point, if more rings are added, no new unique combinations will exist for future rings.
