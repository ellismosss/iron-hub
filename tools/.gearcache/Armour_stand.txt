The '''armour stand''' can be built in the [[repair space]] of the [[workshop]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 55 [[Construction]] to build, granting 500 Construction [[experience]]. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it.

This piece of furniture is useful in its ability to repair armours such as [[Barrows equipment]].

It can also be used to repair [[damaged armour]].

==Repair cost breakdown==
It costs less to repair Barrows or Moons of Peril equipment at an armour stand compared to paying [[Bob]] in [[Lumbridge]]. Stat boosts do affect the price of repair. The formula used to calculate the cost at a repair stand is:

<math>\text{Cost}_\text{AS} = \text{Cost}_\text{NPC} \times \left(1-\frac{\text{Smithing Level}}{200}\right)</math>

A player with a [[Smithing]] level of 60, for instance, would pay 70% of normal cost:

<math>1- \frac{60}{200} = \frac{70}{100}</math>

Simply put, every 2 Smithing levels reduces the repair cost by 1% when compared to an NPC vendor. At level 99 this equals roughly 50% in savings.

[[Damaged armour]] can also be repaired at an armour stand and give a player a platebody of a random metal from bronze to mithril. [[Broken armour]] can also be repaired at the stand and give a player a [[platelegs]] or [[plateskirt]] of random metal, from [[Bronze platebody|bronze]] to [[Mithril platebody|mithril]]. There is a chance with either armour types of breaking it beyond repair.
