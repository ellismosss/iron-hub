A '''portal nexus''' is a room that can be built in a [[player-owned house]]. Only one portal nexus room can be built in a player-owned house. It requires level 72 [[Construction]] and costs {{NoCoins|200000|c}}. Within a portal nexus room, players can build a portal nexus that can [[Teleportation|teleport]] the player to multiple locations around [[Gielinor]]. The cost of adding each teleport to the nexus is 1,000 times the rune cost of that spell. This is 10 times as much as the runes required to direct a [[Portal space]].

The lowest level a player can be to build this room is 67 Construction with an orange [[spicy stew]] (+5 boost), as the +3 boost of the [[crystal saw]] '''only''' applies to furniture built with a [[saw]] and [[hammer]]. A reliable alternative is available at level 69 Construction, as a [[Cup of tea (trimmed)|cup of tea in a trimmed teacup]] provides a visible +3 boost when consumed.

There are 4 [[hotspot]]s available:
*[[Amulet space]]
*[[Curtain space]]
*[[Rug space]]
*[[Portal Nexus space]]

===Adding a teleport===
The portal nexus, like portals in the [[portal chamber]], can add up to 41 possible locations, the limit depending on the tier of the portal nexus built. However, players require 1,000 times the materials required, instead of only 100 times.
*Directing a portal requires the same [[Magic]] level and quest requirements (if any) as casting the teleport spell.
**Unlike the [[Portal Chamber]], [[Magic#Temporary_boosts|temporary magic boosts]] '''cannot''' be used to add portals before reaching the required Magic level.
*Players do not need to be on the same spellbook as the teleport spell they are directing (e.g. they can direct a portal to Kharyrll even if they are on the standard spellbook).
*[[Elemental staves]] and runes in a [[rune pouch]] '''cannot''' be used.
*The house owner does not have to be in building mode to add a destination.
*If a player upgrades their portal nexus, all destinations in it from its previous version are saved. The runes or salt do not have to be provided again.
*Removing a teleport '''does not refund the runes'''. Re-adding the teleport will require "paying" the material cost again.
* No experience is given for adding a teleport to the nexus, unlike when directing portals in the Portal Chamber.

===Customisation and usage===
[[File:Portal Nexus configuration interface.png|thumb|220px|The configuration menu on a portal nexus. The unlocked teleports are displayed in the Slots list.]]
[[File:Portal Nexus teleport interface.png|thumb|220px|The teleport menu on a portal nexus, showing the keyboard options available.]]
Once players have built a portal nexus, they may customise the teleportation interface via the right-click Configuration option. Players will be able to drag and drop the various teleports unlocked into available slots and select which of these acts as the left-click option. The order in which these appear in the Slots list will affect the keyboard options on the right-click Teleport interface.

Within the Teleport Menu interface, a list will be displayed, showing the teleports the player has unlocked, as well as the keyboard options (1-9, A-Z) to use them (which depends on how the player arranged them in the Configuration interface's Slots list). The Teleport Menu interface can also be switched to Scry Mode, which uses the [[scrying pool]]'s function to gaze into areas the player would be teleported to, excluding locations in the [[Wilderness]], where it will instead state the amount of players within the vicinity in the chatbox. To scry outside the house portal, click on the portal nexus icon within the interface.

Once players have paid for a teleport, it will be permanently unlocked to use freely, as long as the portal nexus exists and players keep them in the Slots list. In addition, players can rearrange teleports in the Slots list or change the left click option without any additional cost.

If players were to remove a teleport from the Slots list, remove the portal nexus, or remove the entire room, runes will have to be consumed once more to unlock the teleports when the portal nexus is rebuilt. A confirmation message will appear should players attempt to do so.
