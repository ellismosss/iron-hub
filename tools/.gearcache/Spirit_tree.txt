'''Spirit trees''' are a [[Gnome|gnomish]] method of [[transportation]]. Completion of the [[Tree Gnome Village]] [[quest]] is required for access to the network. Additional completion of [[The Grand Tree]] is required to travel '''from''' the spirit tree at the [[Tree Gnome Stronghold]], but is not required to travel to it. All other trees can be used to travel directly from one tree to another tree.

==Spirit tree locations==
Permanent locations:

*[[Tree Gnome Village (location)|Tree Gnome Village]] in the middle of the [[Gnome Maze]] {{Map|2539,3166|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*[[Tree Gnome Stronghold|Gnome Stronghold]] (requires completion of [[The Grand Tree]] quest to travel from this location. Before the quest, it can still be used as the destination while travelling from other trees). {{Map|2460,3446|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*[[Battlefield|Battlefield of Khazard]] - just south of [[West Ardougne]]. {{Map|2555,3259|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*North-east corner of the [[Grand Exchange]] in [[Varrock]]. {{Map|3185,3510|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*South-west corner of [[Feldip Hills]], next to the [[Myths' Guild]]. {{Map|2486,2850|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*East of the [[Poison Waste]] (requires partial completion of [[The Path of Glouphrie]]) {{Map|2339,3111|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*[[Prifddinas]] (requires completion of [[Song of the Elves]] quest) {{Map|3275,6124|mapID=-1|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}
*[[Laguna Aurorae]] (requires completion of [[Pandemonium]], 58 [[Sailing]], and having moored at the island at least once) {{Map|1202,2786|mapID=-1|type=maplink|mtype=pin|text=}}

Self-grown spirit tree locations '''([[Farming]] only)''':

*In south-eastern [[Etceteria]]. {{Map|2613,3858|type=maplink|mtype=pin|icon=redPin|text=}}
*Easternmost part of [[Port Sarim]]. {{Map|3060,3258|type=maplink|mtype=pin|icon=redPin|text=}}
*North-eastern part of [[Brimhaven]], near the [[Brimhaven Agility Arena|Agility Arena]]. {{Map|2802,3203|type=maplink|mtype=pin|icon=redPin|text=}}
*[[Hosidius]], next to the [[saltpetre]] deposits and south-east of the [[Forthos Ruin]]. {{Map|1693,3542|type=maplink|mtype=pin|icon=redPin|text=}}
*Inside the advanced tier of the [[Farming Guild]]. (''Can be accessed before level 85 with the use of stat boosting items, but requires boosting to 85 everytime teleporting there is desired'') {{Map|1253,3750|type=maplink|mtype=pin|icon=redPin|text=}}

Additionally, with 75 [[Construction]]:

*[[Player-owned house]] [[superior garden]] - The [[crystal saw]] boost cannot be used, but other [[Construction#Temporary boosts|boosts]] are possible. This does not count towards limit on spirit trees planted, and provides only 350 experience in both Farming and Construction.

One of the fastest ways to access a spirit tree is to teleport to the [[Stronghold Slayer Cave]] with a [[Slayer ring]] - the player will arrive close to the tree. For low requirements and no cost, using an [[Ardougne cloak]], one can [[Teleportation|teleport]] to the [[Ardougne Monastery]] and use the [[Battlefield|Khazard Battlefield]] tree just to the north west. The Port Sarim spirit tree patch (83 Farming) is just south of the [[Explorer's ring 2|explorer's ring]]'s (2 or better) cabbage-port. When the player has a [[Farming cape]] or a [[skills necklace]], they can teleport to the [[Farming Guild]] and use the spirit tree there. One of the fastest ways to a spirit tree is by having planted one in a player-owned house (75 [[Construction]] and 83 Farming) and using a [[Teleport to house]] spell or [[magic tablet]]. The fastest way before 83 Farming, is teleporting to the Grand Exchange (with a charged [[ring of wealth]] or teletab with the medium [[Varrock Diary]] completed) and using the spirit tree there.

The fastest way to a spirit tree is using an [[Achievement Diary Cape]] and teleporting to [[Elder Gnome child]]. Once the player has teleported to the Elder Gnome Child, the player will be 9-11 tiles away from a spirit tree. This is faster than the POH spirit tree despite being farther away (no need to load a POH).

==Farming==
{{Main|Spirit Tree (Farming)}}
[[File:Spirit tree icon.png|left]]
Players can grow their own spirit tree from a [[spirit sapling]] made from a [[spirit seed]] once they reach level 83 [[Farming]]. Since spirit seeds and spirit saplings are untradeable, they can be difficult to obtain. [[Farming/Patch locations#Spirit tree patches|Spirit tree patches]] can be found in:
*[[Etceteria]]
*[[Brimhaven]]
*[[Port Sarim]]
*[[Hosidius]]
*[[Farming Guild#Advanced tier|Farming Guild - advanced tier]]

Outside of a POH, players can only have one planted at 83, two at 88, three at 93, and four at 96. Upon reaching 99 Farming, the restriction is removed, and players can plant unlimited spirit trees. These levels can be boosted. 

The growing time for a spirit tree is about 58 hours and 40 minutes. Growing a spirit tree yields 19,501.3 Farming [[experience]], which is the sum of planting (199.5 experience) and checking health (19,301.8 experience).

Spirit trees cannot be cut down, so one simply uses a [[spade]] on the tree to remove it. They do not yield roots when removed.

===Payment===
Spirit trees may be tended by [[gnome]] [[Group of Advanced Gardeners|gardeners]] for 5 [[monkey nuts]], 1 [[monkey bar]], and 1 [[Ground tooth|ground suqah tooth]]. Given the rarity of the seed, the [[experience]] given, and the length of time it takes to mature, having a gardener tend the growing plant is strongly recommended.

===Construction===
[[File:Spirit tree (Construction) icon.png|left]]
Players can plant their own [[spirit tree (Construction)|spirit tree]] from a [[spirit sapling]] in their [[superior garden]] at level 75 [[Construction]]. Doing so also requires level 83 in [[Farming]]. [[Temporary skill boost|Boosts]] can be used. This tree does not count towards the number of spirit trees that may be planted at one time. At level 95 Construction, players can build a [[Spirit tree & fairy ring|Spiritual Fairy Tree]], which grants access to both a spirit tree and a [[Fairy rings|fairy ring]].
