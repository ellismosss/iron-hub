The '''spice rack''' can be built in the [[spice rack space]] of the [[kitchen]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 60 [[Construction]] to build and gives 374 [[experience]] when built.

Players can use the spice rack to store up to 2,147,483,647 doses of [[Brown spice|brown]], [[Orange spice|orange]], [[Red spice|red]], and [[yellow spice]]s. The amount of spices a player withdraws will always be placed into new shakers. It can be interacted with both in and out of building mode.

No other types of spices such as [[gnome spice]] nor [[spice]] can be stored in the spice rack.

If the '''kitchen''' is destroyed and later rebuilt, any spices stored in the spice rack will '''not be lost'''. Rebuilding the spice rack will restore access to the previously stored spices.
