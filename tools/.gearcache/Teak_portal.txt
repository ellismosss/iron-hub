A '''teak portal''' can be built in the [[Portal space]] of the [[Portal Chamber]] in a [[player-owned house]]. It requires 50 [[Construction]] to build and gives 270 [[experience]] when built. The player must have a [[hammer]] and a [[saw]] in their inventory to build it.

It can be assigned to [[teleportation|teleport]] the player to various locations, such as Varrock or Falador. To do so, a payment of 100 times the required [[runes]] for casting the [[teleport]] spell is required.

Removing an old portal frame to upgrade it to a better one will not delete the teleport. The teleport will automatically be reassigned to the new frame.
